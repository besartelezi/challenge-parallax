Pokémon: Starters Adventure

In this game, you play as 3 different Pokémon. The goal of the game is to defeat as many Pokémon as possible!

To defeat an incoming Pokémon, press spacebar. You will shoot out an attack.

But that attack will be of the same type as your Pokémon.

So if you use a fire Pokémon, you will shoot a fire attack.

The only way to defeat an incoming Pokémon, is to hit it with an attack that has an advantage to that Pokémon's type!

Type advantages

Grass defeats Water

Water defeats Fire

Fire defeats Grass

And if you want to know which Pokémon is which type, just play the game and figure it out yourself!
